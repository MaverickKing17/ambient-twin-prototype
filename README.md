**Ambient Twin** is a high-performance, enterprise-grade SaaS designed to modernize the HVAC industry. By creating a "Digital Twin" of residential properties, we connect directly to smart thermostats (Ecobee, Google Nest, Honeywell) to provide real-time telemetry, predictive fault detection, and automated rebate qualifying.

---

## üöÄ Key Features

### 1. AI Predictive Revenue Engine
Utilizes Time-Series Anomaly Detection to identify potential HVAC system failures up to 14 days before they occur, allowing contractors to shift from reactive repairs to proactive service agreements.

### 2. Multi-Protocol IoT Integration
Seamless 2-way sync with leading smart home hardware. Ingests live data including humidity, fan runtime, and system strain to provide a real-time "heartbeat" of the home.

### 3. Web 3.0 Energy Ledger
Every service event and efficiency milestone is recorded on an immutable ledger. Homeowners receive a "Certificate of Efficiency," increasing property resale value and guaranteeing compliance for the $12,000 Enbridge Fuel Switching rebates.

### 4. Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure
- **SOC 2 Type II Ready:** Built with strict audit logging and AES-256 data encryption.
- **RBAC:** Sophisticated Role-Based Access Control for Owners, Dispatchers, and Field Technicians.
- **API-First:** Designed to integrate with ServiceTitan, Jobber, and QuickBooks.
